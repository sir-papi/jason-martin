<p> paragraph is self explanatory. And is nested inside <section>.

And <a> links to freeCodeCamp or whatever is between the <a> tags.

the attribute: target="_blank" within <a> opens hyperlink freeCodeCamp in a new window.

an image: <img src="blah blah blah"> can also be nested w/in an <a> & thus linked to wherever the href designates.

the alt="_" attribute is a best practice, defining an alternative text description of the image. For browser + accessibility purposes.
